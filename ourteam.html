Sinead

PERSONAL TRAINER

Hey guys. My name is Sinéad McElligott! I have just qualified as a personal trainer and I have been lucky enough to have the opportunity of joining the team in One Arena.een.
Fitness has always been a passion of mine. I have first-hand seen the benefits of exercise both physically and mentally, and I decided I wanted to educate people so they too could see these benefits.
On the side, I’m a registered general nurse and have always had a love for health and caring for people.
